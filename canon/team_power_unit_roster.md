**Previous:** "Penalty ballast earned via points performance."  
**Correction:** FIA success ballast applies to constructor championship leaders (not race-by-race winners). Resets each season.  
**Impact:** 
- Mercedes (constructor leader after R2) may carry ballast in later rounds
- Ballast sheds as championship gaps widen (not per-race)
- Betting implication: Penalized teams improve mid-season as ballast load drops relative to performance gain
