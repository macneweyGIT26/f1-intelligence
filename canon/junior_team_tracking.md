## What Each Scenario Signals

### Scenario 1: Lindblad consistently outperforming Hadjar

**What it means:**
- Lindblad is faster learner
- Skill edge separating (not engineering)
- Red Bull pipeline working (good prospect identified)

**Betting action:**
- Week 1–3: Tail plays on Lindblad at high odds ($0.25 on top 10)
- Week 4–6: Increase stakes ($0.50 on top 6) as trend solidifies
- Week 7+: Lindblad is undervalued. Attack his improving odds with bigger allocation.

**Main team signal:**
- Red Bull development process is working
- If RBPT fixes overweight car issue, Verstappen benefits from same engineering improvements

---

### Scenario 2: Hadjar outperforming Lindblad

**What it means:**
- Hadjar is faster learner (or better developed)
- Red Bull is betting on Hadjar as future prospect (not Lindblad)
- Lindblad may be rotating out (older junior program)

**Betting action:**
- Reduce Lindblad exposure
- Don't bet on him as "undervalued discovery"
- Switch focus to Hadjar tail plays

**Main team signal:**
- Red Bull selection is clear. Hadjar getting better setup/pit strategy priority.

---

### Scenario 3: Both performing similarly (narrow gap)

**What it means:**
- Both drivers developing at same rate
- Engineering is the limiter (not driver differentiation)
- Racing Bulls car quality is question mark

**Betting action:**
- Both are mid-tier tail plays (not strong differentiation)
- Skip both until separation emerges

**Main team signal:**
- Red Bull main team likely hitting same engineering ceiling
- If Racing Bulls is slow, Verstappen may be overperforming relative to car

---

### Scenario 4: Mechanical reliability issues (DNFs, pit failures)

**What it means:**
- Racing Bulls reliability is weak
- Could indicate RBPT issues affecting both cars
- Could indicate team pit-crew/strategy issues

**Betting action:**
- Pause tail plays on Racing Bulls drivers
- Monitor if Red Bull main team has same DNF pattern next round
- If yes: System-wide RBPT issue. Affects Verstappen odds.

**Main team signal:**
- If Racing Bulls DNF pattern appears in Red Bull 2 weeks later, it's a power unit issue
- Red Bull's reliability model breaks down mid-season
