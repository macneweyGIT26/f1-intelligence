## HAMILTON UNDERPERFORMANCE AT SUZUKA — Explained (NOT a driver quality issue)

**Root cause:** Overtake mode energy cost + battle with Norris

**Technical breakdown:**
- Hamilton spent energy defending/attacking (overtake mode battery burn)
- Lost slipstream benefit when >1s behind Norris
- Energy spent on race management degraded tire state
- Leclerc had cleaner air, managed better as a result
- **Hamilton pace in clean air remains competitive**

**Betting implication:** Do NOT downgrade Hamilton for Miami based on Suzuka result. It was position-driven, not pace-driven. In clean air at Miami, Hamilton will return to competitive form (expect P4-P6 range if no major Ferrari software gains).
