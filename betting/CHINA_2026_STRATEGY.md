## Core Strategy

**Play Type:** Odds variance arbitrage

1. **Place 2 bets on same driver at different odds** (opening line + 2nd bet at slightly tighter odds)
2. **Monitor odds movement** (typically tighten as event approaches)
3. **Cash out profitable side PRE-RACE** (don't wait for actual result)
4. **Generate returns from line movement, not race outcome**

**Why it works:** Early odds are soft; market reprices as event nears. The gap = edge.
