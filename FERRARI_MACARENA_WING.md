## Technical Innovation

**Designer:** Andrea Galante  
**Key Feature:** Upper flaps rotate up to **270 degrees** (vs. standard ~30-40°)

### What Changed
- **Removed central pylon** (drag reduction)
- **Integrated system into lateral endplates** (structural efficiency)
- **Reshaped third flap** (additional performance gain)

**Benefit:** Active aero flexibility; can transition from low-downforce to high-deploy mode more aggressively.
